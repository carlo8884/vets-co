Hi — I'm here to help you understand your pet's symptoms. What species is your pet, and what are you noticing?
My dog has been lethargic for 2 days and not eating. He's 8 years old.
Thank you. Lethargy + appetite loss lasting 48 hours in a senior dog warrants attention. A few quick questions: Has he vomited or had diarrhea? Any changes in water intake? And is his abdomen visually distended?
⚠️ If the abdomen appears bloated or he seems in pain when touched, this may indicate GDV — a life-threatening emergency. Go to the nearest emergency vet immediately.
