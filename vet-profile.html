About Dr. Reyes

Dr. Amanda Reyes is a board-certified specialist in small animal internal medicine with 14 years of clinical experience. After earning her DVM from UC Davis School of Veterinary Medicine, she completed a three-year internal medicine residency at Cornell University's College of Veterinary Medicine before joining Austin Animal Internal Medicine in 2015.

Dr. Reyes specializes in complex diagnostic cases — particularly chronic diseases of the kidneys, liver, endocrine system, and gastrointestinal tract in dogs and cats. She is known for her clear communication with pet owners and her commitment to evidence-based medicine.

"I believe the most important thing I can do is help owners understand what's happening with their pet and what the options are. Medicine should never feel like a black box."
